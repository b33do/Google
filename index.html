About SNPR Holdings

Practical engineering guidance backed by hands-on experience.

SNPR Holdings (Pty) Ltd is a South African company providing engineering services, procurement and installation, subcontracting support, and construction delivery. With a foundation in computational mathematics and computer engineering, we bridge technical insight with on-site execution to keep projects on schedule and within scope.

Founder and director Rhutlani Ngobeni blends IT leadership and electrical engineering expertise to deliver resilient infrastructure. We coordinate cross-functional teams, manage complex data and network environments, and ensure safe, standards-driven implementation.
